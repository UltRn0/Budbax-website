About Budbax

Budbax is a young cybersecurity startup forged by hard‑working students who believe strong defence should be accessible, transparent and technically rigorous.

From capture‑the‑flag labs to production networks, the team applies research‑grade thinking to practical security problems without the enterprise‑grade price tag.

What we do

The focus is on building reliable security foundations for organisations that are still growing but already serious about protecting data, reputation and users.

Security hardening & vulnerability management
  • System and network security hardening for Linux, Windows and cloud environments
  • Baseline configurations for firewalls, routers, servers and endpoints.
  • Ongoing vulnerability management with clear remediation steps.
Offensive security mindset
  • Student‑style red‑team simulations with clear reporting.
  • Attack path mapping from initial foothold to impact.
  • Hands‑on sessions to train in‑house teams.
Security for students & startups
  • Secure‑by‑design guidance for new products and projects.
  • Low‑budget security baselines for early‑stage companies.
  • Workshops for colleges, clubs and tech communities.
